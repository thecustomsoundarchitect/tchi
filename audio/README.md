# Meditation music files

Five 30-minute classical mixes are included:

- `meditation-1.mp3`: Beethoven, Cello Sonata No. 3, first movement
- `meditation-2.mp3`: Beethoven, Cello Sonata No. 3, second movement
- `meditation-3.mp3`: Beethoven, Cello Sonata No. 3, third movement
- `meditation-4.mp3`: Beethoven, Cello Sonatas No. 1 and No. 2
- `meditation-5.mp3`: Bach and Gounod, Ave Maria for cello and piano

Each mix includes a quiet public-domain forest bird layer. Low-frequency
rumble was filtered from the bird recording. No water, rain, or thunder
recordings were used.
